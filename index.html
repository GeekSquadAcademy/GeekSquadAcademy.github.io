Next Generation of Teens Meets the Next Generation of Tech

Geek Squad Academy from Best Buy provides an opportunity for students to develop tech skills, build self-confidence, spark creativity, and discover how technology can benefit them in their educational pursuits and in future careers.

Students at Geek Squad Academy become "Junior Agents" and get hands-on experience with some of the newest and coolest technology. Local Best Buy employees help the Junior Agents explore areas like:

  • Coding
  • Game Design
  • Photography
  • 3D Design
  • Digital Music
  • ... and more!

It's one thing to tell young people about a possible future in technology, but the real magic happens when you show them that they can start now. Geek Squad Academy is all about creating fun projects with accessible technology that the Junior Agents can show to their friends and family.

Application dates

Geek Squad Academy proposals are open for the 2026 season! Submit a proposal here. Please feel free to reach out to us at academy@geeksquad.com with any questions!

Schools, nonprofits and other community organizations are invited to apply to host Geek Squad Academy during school breaks or as an in-school field trip for youth ages 10 to 18.

Camp Requirements:

  • 60-200 youth attendees

  • Access to 4-7 rooms with space to accommodate up to 25 youth each

  • Common assembly space for lunch and opening/closing ceremonies

  • One adult chaperone for every 20 youth

  • Spaces are available Tuesday for setup and Wednesday-Thursday for camp

We look forward to hearing from you. For questions, please contact academy@geeksquad.com.
