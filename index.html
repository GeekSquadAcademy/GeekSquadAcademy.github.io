Next Generation of Teens Meets the Next Generation of Tech

Geek Squad Academy is designed to give youth ages 10–18 opportunities to develop technology skills that inspire future education and career choices. Through hands-on learning, students envision a future where they are creators, not just consumers, of the technology that powers the world.

When attending a Geek Squad Academy camp, students become Junior Agents and gain experience with some of today’s most exciting technology, led by instructors from Best Buy and Geek Squad. This two-day camp experience introduces students to fields such as coding, robotics, animation, music production, and more.

Application dates

Proposals for Geek Squad Academy are currently closed. We are not accepting new hosting proposals at this time and expect proposals to reopen in October 2026.

Interested in hosting in the future? Complete our Host Interest Form to stay connected. Organizations on the interest list will be notified when proposals reopen and may also be contacted if additional hosting opportunities open up this season.

Please feel free to reach out to us at academy@geeksquad.com with any questions.

Geek Squad Academy partners with schools, nonprofits, and community organizations to host camps during school breaks or as in-school field trip experiences.

Camp Requirements

  • 60–200 youth attendees
  • Access to 4–7 rooms that accommodate up to 25 students each
  • Common assembly space for lunch and opening/closing ceremonies
  • One adult chaperone for every 20 youth
  • Tuesday availability for setup, with camps typically held Wednesday–Thursday

How to Apply

When proposals reopen, hosting organizations will be able to submit an application online through this site. For questions or future planning inquiries, please reach out to academy@geeksquad.com.
