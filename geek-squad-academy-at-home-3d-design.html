Geek Squad Academy at Home – 3D Design

Hello Tech Hungry Civilian!

In this class, we’ll be building a house in Sketchup!  We’ll explore the Shape tools, Push/Pull, offsets, material choices, and if you’re feeling adventurous we’ll even make everything with real-world measurements in feet and inches. This project will take between 30 minutes and one hour depending on how detailed you choose to get, and will only require a computer with internet access, though a mouse is recommended!

SketchUp is an easy 3D modeling program for a wide range of drawing applications similar to those used in industries such as architecture, interior design, engineering, film, and video game design. Learning the basics of this program is a useful first step to understanding the process of creating and working on three-dimensional projects in a variety of possible future careers!

Like all Geek Squad Academy content, this activity was designed for students ages 10 to 18.

Requirements:

  • 1 computer with internet access
  • 1 computer mouse (recommended)
